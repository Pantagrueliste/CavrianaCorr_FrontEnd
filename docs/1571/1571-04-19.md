| **Summary** | Cavriana reports on French-Italian relations, military reforms in France, the arrival of a Turkish ambassador, and court politics. In encrypted passages, he discusses his confidential communications with Catherine de' Medici about the Grand Duke of Tuscany, and offers his services as an intelligence agent. The letter reveals tensions between nobles including Nevers and Guise, and mentions marriage negotiations with Elizabeth I of England. |
